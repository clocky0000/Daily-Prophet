Harry Potter Arrested!

In an unexpected turn of events, the wizarding world is abuzz as Harry Potter was taken into custody early this morning by the Ministry of Magic. Officials state that Potter, renowned for his role in defeating the dark wizard Voldemort, was arrested in connection with the unauthorized use of powerful dark artifacts. Witnesses report that Ministry officials arrived at Potter’s residence at dawn, executing a swift arrest that left the local magical community in shock.

According to sources within the Ministry, several prohibited items were found in Potter’s possession, which could lead to serious charges under the Magical Law Enforcement Act. Minister of Magic, Hermione Granger, expressed her disappointment, emphasizing that no one is above the law, not even heroes of the wizarding world. The arrest raises questions about Potter's current activities and affiliations since his departure from the limelight.

Harry Potter Defends with Illegal Wand!

Chaos erupted last night as Harry Potter used an illegal wand to thwart an attack by dark wizards in Diagon Alley. Eyewitnesses report that Potter was ambushed by a group of masked assailants, believed to be remnants of Voldemort’s followers, intent on causing havoc. With no time to spare, Potter allegedly used a banned wand, capable of highly unpredictable and powerful magic, to defend himself and the bystanders.

The use of such a wand has sparked controversy within the wizarding community, raising concerns about the means Potter is willing to adopt to ensure safety. The Ministry of Magic has launched an investigation into the incident, with officials debating the legality of Potter's actions given the circumstances. Despite the controversy, many citizens have voiced their support for Potter, arguing that his quick thinking prevented what could have been a catastrophic event.
